About Beaver Bill's

Welcome to Beaver Bill's Burger Place, where bold flavors meet hearty portions!
Founded in 2018 in the heart of Bristol by our fearless foodie and burger enthusiast Bill Dawson, we set out on a mission to redefine comfort food. Bill's dream was simple: craft a menu full of quirky, delicious dishes that bring people together and celebrate indulgence with a playful twist.
From our signature Grizzly Beast Burger to our unique Sweet Chilli Veggie Wrap, every item is made with fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a little sprinkle of creativity.
Since opening our first location, we've grown to four bustling restaurants across Bristol, Cardiff, Birmingham Manchester, and London, with big plans to expand even further. Whether you're dining in, taking away, or ordering delivery, you can always count on us for a warm welcome, great food, and an experience that leaves you coming back for more.
Our mission is clear: to serve up mouthwatering meals, create memories, and make sure every bite brings joy to your day. So, grab your friends, your family, or even fly solo—we're here to make your mealtime legendary.
Thank you for being part of the Beaver Bill's family! Stay tuned, because we've got exciting new locations and menu items on the horizon.
